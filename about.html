About Scooter Map

Scooter Map is a web application designed to help residents and visitors of Vienna find scooter parking spots with ease. Developed by a team of mobility enthusiasts, our mission is to facilitate urban transportation and make cities more accessible and environmentally friendly. Our data is regularly updated to ensure accuracy and reliability.

For more information or collaborations, feel free to contact us. Let's make our cities more scooter-friendly together!
